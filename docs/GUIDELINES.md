# PANORA 
## Brand Identity & Visual Guidelines
**Version 1.0.0 | Institutional & B2B Distribution**

---

### PAGE 1: BRAND MANIFESTO & VISION
**End-to-End Visibility.**

**THE MANIFESTO**
The modern food supply chain is fragmented, opaque, and inefficient. PANORA exists to dismantle the silos. We bring an uncompromising, expansive vision to global food logistics. Derived from the word "Panorama," PANORA takes a step back to see the entire network—transforming chaotic distribution webs into clean, uninterrupted, and trackable straight lines. We are not just moving goods; we are building the definitive infrastructure for the future of food security and enterprise distribution. 

**THE VISION**
Total transparency from origin to endpoint. We deliver clinical precision and uninterrupted logistics for a world that demands absolute reliability.

### PAGE 3: LOGO USAGE & CLEAR SPACE RULES
**The Mark of Infrastructure.**

**THE LOGOTYPE**
The PANORA logo is our anchor. Built in our primary typeface (Syne, Extra Bold), it is heavy, structurally sound, and engineered to look like physical infrastructure. 
* **Rule:** The logo must ALWAYS be set in ALL CAPS.
* **Tracking/Kerning:** Letter spacing is heavily tightened to create a unified, block-like presence. 

**CLEAR SPACE**
To maintain our "High-White" aesthetic, the PANORA logo requires massive breathing room. 
* **The "P" Measurement:** The minimum clear space around all sides of the logo must be equal to 200% of the height of the letter "P" in the logotype. No text, graphic elements, or edges of a composition may enter this zone.

**UNACCEPTABLE ALTERATIONS**
* Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logotype.
* Do not use the logo in lowercase.
* Do not apply drop shadows or gradients.

### PAGE 4: COLOR SYSTEM & HIERARCHY
**The 90/10 Rule in Practice.**

Our color palette is heavily restricted. To signal cleanliness, efficiency, and modern tech, 90% of our visual space must utilize our Primary Base. The remaining 10% is reserved for data, critical alerts, and typography.

**PRIMARY BASE / CANVAS (90% Usage)**
* **Pure Horizon (#F9FAFB)**
  * *Purpose:* The fundamental background for all brand materials. It creates the clinical, expansive negative space necessary for our "High-White" aesthetic.

**SECONDARY BASE / UI**
* **Frosted Glass (#F3F4F6)**
  * *Purpose:* Used strictly for subtle UI differentiation (e.g., alternating table rows or inactive interface panels).

**PRIMARY ACCENT / LOGO**
* **Solar Flare (#FF6B00)**
  * *Purpose:* High-energy and disruptive. Used *only* for the PANORA logotype, fleet tracking indicators, or critical system alerts. 

**TEXT / ANCHOR**
* **Deep Obsidian (#111827)**
  * *Purpose:* The heavy anchor for all corporate data, reports, headlines, and UI elements. Provides high contrast against Pure Horizon.

**HIGHLIGHT**
* **Electric Teal (#00D1FF)**
  * *Purpose:* Used sparingly. Strictly reserved for data visualization (charts, graphs) or tech-forward interactive elements in our software.

### PAGE 5: TYPOGRAPHY STACK
**Heavy Infrastructure meets Clean Data.**

**PRIMARY FONT: SYNE (Extra Bold / Bold)**
* *Role:* Logotype, Main Headlines, Slide Titles, and Large Callouts.
* *Rules:* Must ALWAYS be ALL CAPS. Must utilize tight letter spacing. 
* *Concept:* Syne represents the physical weight of our logistics network—the trucks, the warehouses, the infrastructure.

**SECONDARY FONT: INTER (Medium / Regular)**
* *Role:* Body Copy, Sub-headlines, Data Tables, UI Interfaces, and Dashboards.
* *Rules:* Used in standard sentence case. Keep line height generous (1.5) for body copy to maintain legibility.
* *Concept:* Inter is the digital brain of PANORA. It is highly legible, clinical, and perfect for dense supply chain reports and data-heavy environments.

### PAGE 6: LAYOUT SYSTEM & GRAPHIC ELEMENTS
**The Logistics Grid.**

Our layout system relies on mathematical precision. We mimic the look of spreadsheets and data modules to reinforce our analytical approach to logistics.

**THE 90/10 RULE**
90% of the layout must be negative white space (Pure Horizon). Information is clustered into the remaining 10%. Do not fill the page; let the data breathe.

**STRICT GRID ALIGNMENT**
All text must be strictly flush-left or flush-right. No centered text is permitted in body copy or data tables. Elements must snap to a visible or invisible column grid.

**BRAND SHAPES & ELEMENTS**
* **Widescreen Frames:** Use thin, 1pt Deep Obsidian strokes to enclose data modules, imagery, or key statistics. Never use rounded corners.
* **Horizon Lines:** Thick, horizontal dividing lines in Solar Flare. Used to separate major sections or represent supply chain routes crossing the page.
* **Micro-Grids:** A faint, repeating pattern of `+` marks (using Frosted Glass or 10% opacity Deep Obsidian). Placed in the background to subtly represent the vast, interconnected logistics network.

### PAGE 7: BRAND APPLICATION
**Executing in the Real World.**

**1. FLEET & TRUCK LIVERY**
* **Canvas:** The trailer must be entirely Pure Horizon white. 
* **Design:** A massive, tightly-kerned PANORA logo in Deep Obsidian spans the side. A single, thick Solar Flare "Horizon Line" runs horizontally across the bottom edge of the trailer. No complex graphics. Clean, disruptive, unmissable.

**2. B2B PITCH DECKS**
* **Layout:** Employ the 90/10 rule. A slide should contain no more than one primary headline (Syne, ALL CAPS) and one module of data.
* **Style:** Use Widescreen Frames to box in charts. Use Electric Teal for data visualization. Backgrounds must remain strictly Pure Horizon. 

**3. SOFTWARE DASHBOARDS & UI**
* **Navigation:** Left-aligned or right-aligned menus. Strict grid adherence.
* **Color Usage:** Backgrounds are Pure Horizon. Data tables utilize Frosted Glass for row separation. Critical supply chain delays or alerts ping in Solar Flare. Interactive elements hover in Electric Teal. Text remains Deep Obsidian.
